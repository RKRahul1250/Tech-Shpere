About

  • 2009-2011

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Tech Sphere was born with a passion for providing custom web development and IT consulting services to small businesses. What started as a two-person venture quickly grew as we earned the trust of local clients with reliable and scalable solutions.

  • March 2011

    An Agency is Born

    Recognizing the demand for holistic IT services, we officially became Tech Sphere Solutions, offering a complete suite of services, including cloud migration, security architecture, and custom enterprise applications. Our commitment to innovation drove client success across industries.

  • December 2015

    Transition to Full Service

    We embraced cutting-edge technologies, integrating AI-powered solutions, predictive analytics, and machine learning models into our projects. Our work transformed traditional IT infrastructures into dynamic and intelligent ecosystems.

  • July 2020

    Phase Two Expansion

    Amid a world shifting toward digital transformation, Tech Sphere expanded globally, establishing partnerships across North America, Europe, and Asia-Pacific. Our teams now deliver 24/7 cloud support, cybersecurity services, and data-driven automation solutions to enterprises of all sizes.
